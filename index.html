About Us:

Angel Failure are an Australian metalcore band. The band consists of vocalist Thomas Hughes, guitarists Lachlan Manson and Connor Sabo, bassist Ben Mckay and drummer Brandon Ferringo. They released their self-titled debut EP 'Angel Failure' on 3 November 2017 to critical acclaim and it was nominated for the 2018 ARIA Award for Best Hard Rock. On 14 November 2019 Angel Failure announced their debut album 'The Reckoning' and released their first single from the album named 'Summoner'. The album is set to be released in the summer of 2021.

The band has also announced an upcoming tour in 2021 alongside friends and peers 'Polaris' and 'Thy Art is Murder'. The tour will take place across all major Australian cities including Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth, Melbourne and Sydney.
